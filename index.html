Welcome to MyOwnLatte

At our coffe shop, we believe that every cup of coffee should be as unique as you are. That’s why we offer you the chance to create your own custom latte!

Choose your base:

Pick your favorite milk from our diverse selection, including classic options like whole milk and skim milk, as well as plant-based alternatives like almond, soy, or oat milk.

Select your coffee:

Whether you love a bold espresso or a smooth brew, our baristas are here to help you find the perfect blend that will awaken your taste buds.

Add a sweet touch:

Complete your creation with our artisanal syrups available in a variety of flavors, from vanilla to hazelnut, along with seasonal options that will delight your senses.

A moment to savor:

Whether you're looking for a moment of relaxation or an energy boost, every sip of your custom latte is an invitation to enjoy a coffee made just for you.

Come explore the endless possibilities and unleash your creativity in a warm and friendly atmosphere. We can’t wait to see you craft your perfect latte!
